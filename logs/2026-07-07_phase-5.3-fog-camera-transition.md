# Phase 5.3 — Coil fog, camera knots, arbor→coil transition, descent harness

**Date:** 2026-07-07

Stage 5.3 of the coil band: the fog handoff, the band's camera choreography
(the first knots authored past depth 0.43), the crossfade envelopes on both
scenes, and a new isolated harness that verifies transitions without ever
loading the site.

## What was done

- **`coil-fog.ts` (+7 tests):** pure additive fog layer mirroring
  arbor-fog.ts — density delta peaks 0.014 at depth 0.44 (window
  [0.42, 0.50], the materialization haze) and a tint blend toward the
  band's blue-slate anchor `#2b3038` (max 0.5, gone by 0.58, its real work
  in the 0.42–0.47 navy→blue crossover). Products of smoothsteps — exactly
  zero outside the windows; tested disjoint from the arbor density window.
  Composed in `scene-atmosphere.tsx` after the arbor terms (~6 lines);
  `fog-density.ts` untouched.
- **Camera knots:** four knots inserted between the 0.43 hold and the 1.0
  tail — 0.455 (dive from the hold, first beads glimmer below), 0.48
  (broadside resolve, reduced anchor), 0.52 (orbit behind/below — reads as
  thread parallax in motion), 0.565 (settle, target pulled past the cluster
  so it rests on the LEFT third with annotation room right; reduced anchor).
  All existing keyframe invariant tests (C1 continuity, strictly increasing)
  pass unmodified. Framing designed around `COIL_ORIGIN [0, -26, -40]`.
- **Arbor exit fade (`ArborMesh.tsx`):** envelope 0.445→0.485 multiplied
  into the trunk's reveal and written to the three glow materials' unused
  `uOpacity` (verified consumed in all four shaders) — the tree dissolves
  into the deepening haze and is gone before the 0.49 registry unmount.
- **Coil linker reveal (`CoilMesh.tsx`):** threads get an authored
  lights-first envelope 0.435→0.455 ahead of the bead mass (0.44→0.48); the
  always-on linker can no longer show through the 0.37–0.43 mount overlap.
- **Descent-preview harness (`descent-preview.html` +
  `src/dev/descent-preview.tsx`):** the real Canvas stack (SceneAtmosphere +
  SceneManager + CameraController + PostFX) isolated with no document layer.
  Hidden per-band stubs feed the theme bridge so fog/clear colors track
  depth exactly as live; intro store finished pre-mount (deep-link
  precedent). Leva: full camera knot panel (bake → clipboard) + a descent
  folder (depth slider, linear sweep buttons). URL params:
  `?depth/?from/?to/?dur/?dpr/?reduced/?perf`; scripted hooks `__setDepth`,
  `__sweep`, `__preview`. Dev-only — not referenced by index.html, so never
  built.

## Verification (descent preview ONLY, dpr 2)

- Beat walk 0.41 → 0.565: arbor hold → dive, haze thickens, arbor exits
  frame-top mid-dissolve → cluster ghost glimmers center → resolved
  broadside at 0.48 → orbit below → left-third settle. Reverse scrub
  frames match forward exactly (pure functions of depth).
- Crossfade window: arbor at 0 opacity before its unmount — no pop; no
  frame with both scenes at awkward half-presence center-frame.
- Perf: 60.3 fps through a 6 s linear sweep 0.40→0.60; draw calls/frame
  15 (arbor band), 14 (crossfade overlap), 10 (coil mid-band).
- Reduced motion (`?reduced=1`): depth 0.5 cuts to the 0.48 anchor, 0.55
  to the 0.565 settle; frame pixel-stable over 800 ms (drift/shimmer/motes
  frozen).
- Gates: typecheck, lint, 183 tests (23 files), build; bundle contains no
  descent-preview/r3f-perf/leva identifiers.
- One knot iteration was done by eye (the settle was dead-center; retuned
  to the left third). Camera-keyframe source edits need a page reload in
  the harness — HMR leaves a stale `liveCameraKeyframes` clone.
