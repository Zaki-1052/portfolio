# Phase 5.1 — Coil generator, mesh, and preview harness

**Date:** 2026-07-06

Third-band 3D scene, stage 5.1 plus the isolated preview harness. A dense
solenoid cluster of oblate beads on a threaded fiber, in the band's rose
register. Built to the graphics-first pattern the arbor scene established:
pure seeded generator → merged hand-built BufferGeometry → drei
`shaderMaterial` with hand-rolled lighting + manual exp2 fog → mesh with
imperative per-frame uniform writes → thin scene component in the registry.

## What was done

- **`src/utils/coil-generator.ts` (+ test, 13 tests):** pure, zero-import,
  seeded (`mulberry32` duplicated per the foundation-layer precedent). Emits a
  flat `CoilNode[]`: solenoid placement at equal angle steps (constant
  arc-length on a helix), seeded jitter clamped against natural spacing, a
  forward-sweep spacing floor (no interpenetration at any seed), twist-free
  parallel-transport frames, two marked regions, and per-bead unwound morph
  targets (identity for unmarked beads). Exports `generateCoil`,
  `unwindRegion` (Approach-B seam), `regionBeadIndices`, `regionAnchor`,
  `loopArcPairs`, `COIL_GROWTH_DEFAULTS`.
- **`src/scales/chromatin/coil-geometry.ts`:** `buildBeadGeometry` (merged
  oblate spheroids, orientation baked from the transport frame, morph centers
  in `aCompactPos`/`aUnwoundPos`, manual bounding sphere over the compact ∪
  unwound union) and `buildLinkerGeometry` (sagging Bézier tubes, honest
  bounding sphere). A hann-windowed `aLocusW` gives each region one soft
  hotspot.
- **Shaders `src/scales/chromatin/shaders/coil-{bead,linker}.{vert,frag}`:**
  bead vert carries the `uUnwindBlend`/`uFocusRegion` morph (parked at 0/−1
  this stage — provable no-op) behind an `#ifdef USE_INSTANCING` seam; bead
  frag does hemisphere+wrapped-key+rose-rim shading with fbm groove bands;
  linker is additive glow with a traveling shimmer.
- **Params / materials / live-params / mesh / scene:** `coil-params.ts`
  (look params, `COIL_ORIGIN`, tight/open/loose presets), `coil-materials.ts`,
  subscribable `coil-live-params.ts`, `CoilMesh.tsx`, `ChromatinScene.tsx`
  (adds an inlined sparse cool-rose drift field). Registered in
  `scene-manager.tsx`.
- **Dev harness:** `coil-dev-tools.tsx` (leva growth+look folders, presets),
  `chromatin-preview.tsx` + `chromatin-preview.html`, DEV-gated lazy mount in
  `app.tsx`.

## Decisions made

- **Merged BufferGeometry for the bead layer, not InstancedMesh** (user call).
  The `#ifdef USE_INSTANCING` guard keeps the design doc's InstancedMesh
  rewrite a swap of `buildBeadGeometry` + the mesh JSX only — shader contract
  untouched. **User reserved the right to switch to the InstancedMesh version
  if the merged look disappoints.**
- Morph plumbing built now so geometry needs no second pass in 5.4.
- Locus glow reworked mid-session from a flat per-bead term (washed out under
  bloom) to a rim-weighted single hotspot per region.

across seed changes. Production build excludes the preview/leva chunks.
