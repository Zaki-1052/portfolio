## Decisions made

- Fixed-turns/pinned-entry winding replaces the planned compensated-`round()`
  scheme — continuity by construction beat mitigation (risk #1 deleted).
- `wrapTurns` fractional part ≈ 0.6 aims each exit at the next drum (entry
  faces the previous one) → short tidy bridges; default 1.6.
- Thread/knob lighting reuses the drums' exact environment (one light rig);
  warmth lives in albedo + emissive only.
- User look choices (lit+glow thread, amber knobs, 6-turn column) are
  STARTER DEFAULTS on live sliders, not locks.
