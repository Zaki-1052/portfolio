## What was done

**3.6-D — fog + effects (user: "the missing piece").** The composer was never broken — it was starved. Three fixes: `lookFor(depth)` (`look-curve.ts`) drives `uLook` 0.7→0.25 so the surface finally crosses the bloom threshold; the fog density curve gained an opening **veil segment** (lands at 0.011, parts to 0.006 over depth 0–0.06 — the establish reveal) and an **interior swell** (0.014→0.06 across the transit, settling by 0.31); `--fog-color` lifted to warm haze for approach/tissue. Interior: aperture-keyed light (`lightK`, floor 0.30) — light pours from the opening, hierarchy without the abyss. New `atmosphere-dev-tools` leva panel + `atmosphere-live-params` override channel (the tuning instrument; panels stay while friends review).

**Void-fillers (fog needs a body — scene fog only tints geometry).** Five layers, 5 draw calls, all keyed to the live fog color: `atmosphere-halo` (glow quad behind the form — the silhouette floats in luminous haze), `atmosphere-motes` dust (800), `atmosphere-clouds` (10 wispy normal-blended patches, one merged bank — they genuinely veil the form), `atmosphere-shafts` (3 exterior rakes + 1 interior aperture beam, axial-billboarded, one bank), `AtmosphereEmbers` (110 rising sparks in the cavern; shared DriftField shader with the dust).

**3.7 — Overture.** `intro.ts` store (typing→push→done; push gated on typingDone && sceneReady — the coil bake landing is the honest ready signal), `intro-typing.ts` pure math, `LoadingSequence.tsx` overlay (gold mono, `--z-intro: 20` under `--z-nav`), `INTRO_KEYFRAMES` (start 2.5× out along the establish bearing — the form emerges from the veil; end knot spreads `CAMERA_KEYFRAMES[0]`, handoff asserted to 1e-10). Scroll locked at boot, released at landing; `#hash` deferred to landing. Reduced motion / WebGL fallback: typing → cut, no push, no hang.

**Boot-position bug (user-reported lock).** Browser scroll restoration / native hash anchors booted the page mid-descent behind the overlay → first scene unmounted → no bake → `sceneReady` never fired. Fix: `history.scrollRestoration = 'manual'` + forced `lenis.scrollTo(0)` before the lock — the overture always plays from the top. User confirmed fixed.

**3.8 — Parallax + shafts + embers.** `pointer-parallax.ts`: exact closed-form critically-damped spring (dt-robust, no overshoot), applied in `camera-controller` as rotation-only post-multiply (±1.7°/±1.3°), epsilon-settled invalidation, off under reduced/intro.

**3.9 — Integration.** `ASYM_HEIGHT` promoted to `uAsymmetry`/`asymmetry` slider (per-half mirror-breaking, was a buried const 0.35); `dissolveRadius` slider wired (stalk mirrors it); post-hero spacer → named `.runway-post-hero` class (height LOCKED to camera timing).

## Decisions
- **The void needs geometry to carry fog** — halo/clouds/shafts ARE the visible atmosphere; density alone can never fill empty clear-color space.
- Overture boot invariant: **always from the top**; the hash is honored at the landing.
- Panels stay through the dev/tuning period (DEV-gated — production never ships them).
- Intro palette: **gold** (user-picked); green/blue A/B scaffolding removed after the decision.
