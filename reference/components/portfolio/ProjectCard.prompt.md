Featured project card: title-as-link, one sentence, tags. Use at a scale's content phase and (with `glass`) pinned near dendrite branch tips over the 3D scene.

```jsx
<ProjectCard
  title="Chromatin conformation analysis (Hi-C)"
  href="https://github.com/Zaki-1052/mariner_hi-c"
  description="Replicate-aware differential loop calling on BAP1-knockout mouse cerebellum."
  tags={["R", "mariner", "edgeR", "Hi-C"]}
  meta="Ferguson Lab"
/>
```

Hover tints the border and title accent and reveals an ↗ on the title; no scaling. `glass` adds a light backdrop blur for over-canvas cards (use sparingly). Radius follows the active scale.
